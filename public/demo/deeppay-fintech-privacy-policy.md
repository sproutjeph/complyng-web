# DeepPay Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Entity:** DeepPay Limited, Lagos, Nigeria

DeepPay Limited ("DeepPay", "we", "us") provides payment and lending
services to Nigerian consumers and businesses. This Privacy Policy explains
how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information.

## 1. Scope

This policy covers personal information processed via our mobile app, website,
and customer support channels. It applies to all DeepPay customers and
visitors to our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

- **Identity data** — full name, date of birth, Bank Verification Number (BVN),
  National Identification Number (NIN), government-issued ID images, selfie
  photographs.
- **Contact data** — residential address, email, phone number.
- **Financial data** — bank account numbers, transaction history, debit card
  details, loan application details.
- **Device data** — IP address, device identifiers, app version, location at
  login.
- **Usage data** — session logs, feature engagement, referral source.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

- Create and administer your DeepPay account
- Verify your identity under Nigeria's KYC rules
- Process payments and disburse loans
- Detect and prevent fraud and money-laundering activity
- Respond to customer support requests
- Send product updates and marketing messages (with your consent)
- Comply with applicable laws, including NDPR 2019 and GAID 2025

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases under the NDPR 2019:

- **Consent** — for marketing communications and optional features.
- **Performance of a contract** — to provide you with the services you
  requested.
- **Legal obligation** — for KYC, tax, and financial reporting.
- **Legitimate interest** — to prevent fraud and improve our services.

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share personal information with:

- Licensed payment processors and banks to execute transactions
- Credit bureaus for loan risk assessment
- Regulators such as CBN, NDPC, EFCC, and FIRS when legally required
- Technology service providers (cloud hosting, analytics, customer support)
  under appropriate data processing agreements

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

## 6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

DeepPay uses cloud infrastructure operated by providers based in the
United States and the European Union. Where we transfer personal data outside
Nigeria, we rely on contractual safeguards with those providers.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you maintain an account
and for a reasonable period thereafter to meet our legal obligations.
Transaction records are kept for at least seven years in line with CBN
AML/CFT requirements.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications
- Request deletion in certain circumstances
- Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission

To exercise these rights, email privacy@deeppay.ng.

## 9. Security

We protect your information using encryption in transit and at rest,
role-based access controls, and regular security reviews. Despite these
measures, no system can be guaranteed fully secure.

## 10. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be
communicated via in-app notifications or email.
